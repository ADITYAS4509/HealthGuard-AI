Coverage Types

OPD vs. IPD Coverage

Understanding which type of care applies to your condition determines how your claim can be processed.

OPD
Outpatient Coverage
OPD (Outpatient Department) covers consultations, diagnostic tests, and medications where the patient is seen and sent home without formal admission.
IPD — Recommended
Inpatient Coverage
IPD (Inpatient Department) requires formal hospital admission for at least 24 hours. Most standard health insurance policies are designed primarily for IPD claims.
